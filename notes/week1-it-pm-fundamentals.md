# Week 1 — IT Project Management Fundamentals (notes)

## What I’m practicing
- Writing scope in plain language (in-scope vs out-of-scope)
- Defining “done” with measurable success criteria
- Keeping risks visible early (not after things go wrong)

## Simple checklist I can reuse
- Who is the sponsor and who approves scope changes?
- What is the enforcement/cutover date (if any)?
- What are the top 3 risks and how will I reduce them?
- How will I report status weekly (format + audience)?
